Welcome, to me

Hi, I'm a Interface Developer currently working in Sydney for Orchard Marketing. I'm pretty good at XHTML, CSS, Web Standards, Accessibility and all that kind of stuff. I've also been known to dabble with ASP.Net(C#) for a couple of years and still like to keep it old school with some classic ASP (VB Script), but I wouldn't say it's my first love.
